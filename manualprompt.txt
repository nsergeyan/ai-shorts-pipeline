WHAT MAKES A FACT GOOD (READ FIRST)
═══════════════════════════════════
The target is NOT "most obscure." The target is "makes a viewer say wait, WHAT?"

Obscurity is allowed and welcome — but ONLY when it raises the reaction. A rare-but-boring fact is worse than a mid-rarity fascinating one.

The ideal fact pattern (the "soap pattern"):
A small, concrete, almost ordinary question (a texture, smell, sound, habit, weird detail) applied to a famous or central thing — where the real, canon answer is funnier or stranger than expected.

Reference example (from another series, for calibration only):
"Sukuna's severed finger feels and smells like soap." → famous object, hidden physical detail, canon, zero lore needed, instant 'wait WHAT?'. THIS is the level and flavor to aim for.

TOPIC DISCOVERY PROTOCOL (MANDATORY)
═══════════════════════════════════
Generate FIVE candidate ideas in the chosen category.
Each idea must name a SPECIFIC source anchor at this stage (chapter, episode, fanbook page, or interview) — vague ideas are discarded immediately.

For each candidate assign ONE score only:

WAIT-WHAT SCORE (one–five):
one → "ok, neat"
two → "mildly interesting"
three → "huh, I didn't know that"
four → "oh that's actually wild"
five → "wait, WHAT? — I want to tell someone right now"

A fact must score FOUR or higher to be used.

KILL TEST — automatically reject a candidate if it is:
- well-known to most fans (no surprise)
- obscure but DRY (stat numbers, IQ figures, voice-actor credits, staff names, release dates, episode-count records) — rare yet boring
- requires a lore lecture before the surprise lands
- philosophical interpretation or character summary
- a character trait or personality quirk reworded as a surprising fact (e.g. "Gojo is actually really competitive" — this is characterization, not a fact)
- more than forty percent similar to anything in the USED VIDEOS LIST

BANNED OVERSATURATED PATTERNS (the iceberg clichés — never pick these):
character IQ/stat numbers, voice-actor trivia, "the author originally planned…", hidden background text/languages, episode-count or runtime records, sleep/eating habits stated as a character's whole personality.

Pick the highest-scoring surviving candidate.
If no candidate scores four or higher, generate five NEW ideas.

── YOUTUBE QUERIES (for sourcing raw footage) ────────────────
Generate exactly six YouTube queries. Each query must target a DIFFERENT source type to maximize your chance of finding at least three clean, editable clips. Use the strategies below in this order:

Query one — OFFICIAL SOURCE
Target: MAPPA's official YouTube channel uploads, official trailers, or official clips.
Format: jujutsu kaisen official [scene description] MAPPA
Goal: pull footage released by the studio itself — lowest copyright friction.

Query two — CREDITLESS / ISOLATED FOOTAGE
Target: creditless openings, creditless endings, or character highlight reels without commentary.
Format: jujutsu kaisen [character or arc name] [NC / creditless / raw / no commentary] [action]
Goal: clean isolated footage with no streamer face, no commentary audio, no reaction overlay.

Query three — FAN COMPILATION (visuals only)
Target: fan-made AMV or scene compilation focused on the visual moment, no full-episode rips.
Format: jujutsu kaisen [character or power name] [visual moment] AMV / compilation
Goal: find footage that shows the moment from multiple angles or cuts, already trimmed.

Query four — SPECIFIC EPISODE / ARC CLIP
Target: uploads focused on a specific episode number or arc name that contains the relevant scene.
Format: jujutsu kaisen [episode number or arc name] [scene description] clip / scene
Goal: pull from full-episode or arc-specific uploads — different pool from compilations.

Query five — CHARACTER TRIBUTE / SHOWCASE
Target: tribute videos or character showcase edits dedicated to this character, not a general AMV.
Format: jujutsu kaisen [character name] tribute / best moments / character edit
Goal: tribute edits tend to focus tightly on the character with clean cuts and no commentary.

Query six — 4K / HD REMASTER
Target: upscaled or remastered uploads of the relevant scene or episode.
Format: jujutsu kaisen [character or scene] 4K / HD remaster / upscale
Goal: remastered uploads are often clean, long, and watermark-free — a different pool from standard rips.

SCENE ACCURACY RULE: If the script references a specific moment, all six queries must chase that exact moment — not the arc, not the character in general.

DO NOT generate queries that are slight rephrasings of the same idea. Each must point to a genuinely different pool of results.
